SuperStore Sales Insight Generation

Data Cleaning & Transformation To begin the Superstore Sales Analysis project, I performed thorough data cleaning and transformation to ensure the dataset was analysis-ready. This involved identifying and handling missing values, removing duplicates, standardizing date formats, and converting data types where necessary. I also created calculated columns such as order year, month, and profit margin to enhance analytical depth. Shipping delays and return status were flagged using conditional logic to support downstream diagnostic analysis. These transformations provided a clean and structured foundation for building a robust data model.


Data Modelling Using Power BI, I designed a star schema that included multiple dimension tables—such as Customers, Products, Regions, Shipping Modes, and Calendar—linked to a central FactSales table. This dimensional modeling approach improved query performance and supported flexible slicing and dicing across multiple metrics. Relationships were established using primary and foreign keys, and appropriate cardinality was enforced. Measures were created using DAX for KPIs such as Total Sales, Total Profit, AOV, and Profit Margin. The model was optimized with hierarchies and column indexing to facilitate seamless navigation and aggregation.


Data Visualization & Insights I developed an interactive dashboard to answer both descriptive and diagnostic business questions. KPI cards were used for high-level metrics such as Total Sales, Profit, Orders, Customer Count, AOV, and Profit Margin, often with sparklines to show trends. Visuals included bar charts for top products and sales by sub-category, filled maps for regional performance, stacked charts for shipping costs by mode, and gauges for margin tracking. For diagnostics, I built visuals to highlight loss-making products, regions with negative profits, and return rates by category. Drill-through filters allowed deeper exploration into customer segment profitability and the impact of shipping delays on financial outcomes. This BI solution empowered stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions across sales, marketing, and logistics functions.
